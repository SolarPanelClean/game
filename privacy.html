🌞 Solar Clean — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Solar Clean ("the Game") is developed by Yaniv Waldman (ninja-niv.com). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our game.

1. Information We Collect

Solar Clean collects minimal information:

  • Player name: The name you enter before playing is stored locally on your device only (using browser localStorage). It is never sent to any server.
  • Game scores: If you submit a high score to the leaderboard, your player name and score are stored in our database (Supabase) to display on the global leaderboard.
  • Daily progress: Your daily panel count and streak are stored locally on your device only.

2. Advertising

Solar Clean may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and device identifiers to show personalized ads based on your interests. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, email address, or phone number
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect payment information
  • We do not share your data with third parties (except Google AdSense as described above)

4. Children's Privacy

Solar Clean is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The only data stored is the player nickname entered voluntarily before gameplay.

5. Data Storage & Security

Leaderboard scores (player nickname + score) are stored securely using Supabase cloud database. Local game data is stored only on your device and can be cleared by clearing your browser/app data.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Clear all local game data by clearing your browser or app cache
  • Request removal of your leaderboard entry by contacting us

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
